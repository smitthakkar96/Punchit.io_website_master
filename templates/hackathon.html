About Hack404

What is a Hackthon?

Hackthon is a all about team work. Idea generators and coders collaborate to make their idea in to a promising product at the end of hackthon Idea is pitched to the mentors which are further converted to startups.

I'm a novice, is this hackthon for me?

Yes, beside coding there are various workshops, events and marvellous mentors to help you out in boosting up.

Whar is Hack404?

It is a 30 hours hackthon in which, teams have to work on their product each team consist of maximum three members. At last ideas are pitched to the mentors best ideas will be taked up for investment and incubation.
